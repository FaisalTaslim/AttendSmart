Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to the Login Page and click on “Forgot Password.” You’ll receive a reset link on your registered email.

I didn’t receive my login credentials. What should I do?
  • Please check your spam, or promotions folder on your registered email.
  • If you still can't find your credentials:
    • Go to Issue Submission Form, and submit your problem.
    • Do not forget to include:
      • Your Organization Name
      • Your Roll No: For Students
      • Your Employee ID: For Employees
I paid for the premium, but I didn't get the access to the software.

We're sorry for the inconvenience! Please reach out to support@attendsmart.io. Use your registered email to send us a mail. Add a subject matching to your problem, i.e., "Didn't receieve the premium access". Include your Organization name, your Employee ID, and a photo proof of the amount you paid for the premium. Then wait for the reply from our end!

Can multiple branches or departments use AttendSmart under one organization?

Yes! AttendSmart is designed to be universal and scalable. So multiple branches under the same organization can register and get started with AttendSmart.

What platforms does AttendSmart work best on?

AttendSmart is currently optimized for desktops and smartphones. While tablets will work just fine, we still recommend avoiding tablet use for the best experience for now. Full Tablet compatibility and certain features are still under development. We appreciate your patience and support. Stay tuned — exciting updates are on the way! Keep your eyes out on AttendSmart LogBook to track changes made by developers.

How can I report proxy or fake attendance?

AttendSmart is built with features to minimize proxy attendance through secure tracking methods. However, if someone bypasses the system by logging into another user's account, the responsibility for monitoring and disciplinary action lies with the respective organization.

To help with this, AttendSmart provides advanced tools like:

  • Dashboard activity logs to trace user behavior
  • Suspend / Freeze features to temporarily restrict access or halt attendance tracking for specific users

These features are designed to empower admins and maintain transparency in attendance records.

How can I delete my account?

If you're a student or employee, account deletion requests must be handled by your organization's admin. For security and compliance reasons, only admins have the authority to manage or remove user accounts. Please contact your respective admin for assistance.

Can I suggest a feature?

Absolutely! We love new ideas. Use the Feedback Form on the Support page to send us your suggestions.

Is AttendSmart free or paid?

AttendSmart is currently a paid software. We’re a small, independent team, and every contribution goes directly into maintaining the platform and building better features for our users. Your support helps us grow, improve, and keep things running smoothly.

How secure is my data?

Your data security is important to us. We follow industry-standard practices for encryption and access control. More security layers will be added in future updates.
